# Code of Conduct

We’re building in public. Be kind, direct, and professional.

## We expect
- Respectful communication
- Constructive feedback (criticize ideas, not people)
- Safe collaboration across cultures and backgrounds

## We don’t allow
- Harassment, hate speech, or targeted abuse
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Sexual content or unwanted advances
- Threats or encouragement of harm

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content, close threads, or restrict participation if behavior breaks this code.
To report issues, contact maintainers via the security/contact route in `SECURITY.md`.
